## Design System

### Brand Colors (Reifenmeister)
- Primary Red: `#D32F2F` (CTAs, highlights)
- Primary Blue: `#0D47A1` (links, icons, accents)

### Design Principles
- Clean, professional appearance
- High contrast black/white foundation
- Strategic use of brand colors
- Mobile-responsive
- Fast loading times
- No fancy animations or excessive decorations

## Key Decisions
- NO appointment booking system
- Cookie consent popup (localStorage)
- Contact via phone/email only
- Static site (no backend)
